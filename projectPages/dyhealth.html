DE YANG HEALTH RESORT

PROJECT TYPE: HOSPITALITY | LOCATION: CHENGDU, CHINA | SIZE: 300,000 SF| STATUS: PROPOSAL

Inspired by the timeless beauty of the traditional Chinese courtyard, the De Yang Health Resort welcomes its patrons to a serene Zen garden, creating a harmonious retreat. Each wing enveloping the garden houses resort amenity programs, fostering an uninterrupted physical and visual connection to nature for all occupants. The expansive roofs, gracefully supported by columns, evoke the tranquility of tree canopies in lush forests. The choice of wood construction combined with glass amplifies the immersive experience, seamlessly blending the indoors with the natural surroundings.

Beyond the central resort house, the ongoing main axis is flanked by individual resort residences strategically positioned to optimize the site's advantages. The grounded dwellings are gracefully encircled by reflective pools, while the elevated residences stand on pilotis, aligning with the height of the majestic tree canopies. This thoughtful design not only pays homage to traditional Chinese architecture but also creates a holistic haven where every element, from architecture to landscape, contributes to a rejuvenating and immersive experience for the guests of the De Yang Health Resort.
