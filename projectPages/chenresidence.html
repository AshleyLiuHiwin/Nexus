CHEN RESIDENCE

PROJECT TYPE: RESIDENTIAL | LOCATION: QUEENS, NY | SIZE: 5,000 SF| STATUS: COMPLETED

The Chen's Residence, a 5,000-square-foot two-family house, features a striking main double-height vestibule that serves as a shared focal point for both families. The ground floor unit provides access to the cellar and backyard, while the second floor unit enjoys its distinct living space. Each thoughtfully designed unit encompasses three bedrooms, a well-appointed bathroom, and an open kitchen seamlessly integrated with the inviting living room. This architectural gem harmoniously balances individual privacy and communal living, creating a haven of comfort, style, and thoughtful design for both families to enjoy.
