EAST WILLIAMSBURG

PROJECT TYPE: MIXED USE | LOCATION: BROOKLYN, NY | SIZE: 2,580,000 SF| STATUS: PROPOSAL

The East Williamsburg Development unfolds on a sprawling 580,000-square-foot site along the Newtown Creek in Brooklyn, New York City. Nestled in an industrial enclave, this neighborhood resonates with the vibrant creative energy of local artists and musicians. Drawing inspiration from iconic districts like SOHO and DUMBO, the East Williamsburg Development aspires to embody the neighborhood's distinctive artistic culture at the core of its development strategy.

Three towers gracefully emerge from the site, each serving a unique purpose. One tower is exclusively dedicated to artist live-work studios and gallery spaces, fostering a creative hub. Another tower functions as a transient hotel, welcoming those intrigued by the local culture, while the third stands as a beacon of luxury condominium living. Interconnecting strips of commercial spaces and verdant recreational areas elegantly bridge the trio of towers, creating a seamless flow.

Public plazas and a marina enliven the waterfront, providing vibrant spaces for communal interaction. Aligned with New York City's Vision 2020 plan to enhance the city's waterfront, the East Williamsburg Development exemplifies a design philosophy that harmonizes with its surroundings. As part of its commitment to sustainability, the development pursues a net-zero site, diligently addressing energy use and emissions. Innovative strategies, including ground-sourced heat exchange, daylight and solar control, and separate, distributed air supply and heating/cooling, contribute to its environmentally conscious approach. This visionary development not only transforms the physical landscape but also captures the spirit of East Williamsburg's artistic identity, enriching the urban fabric and setting a new standard for waterfront living in the heart of Brooklyn.
