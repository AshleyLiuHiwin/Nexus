WEST SEAPORT HOTEL

PROJECT TYPE: HOSPITALTIY | LOCATION: XIAMEN, CHINA | SIZE: 500,000 SF| STATUS: PROPOSAL

A premium hotel is envisioned for a significant seaport terminal located on the western shores of Xiamen, China. The primary design challenge revolves around seamlessly integrating the hotel and amenity programs without obstructing the panoramic views of the existing luxury residential towers inland. In response, Studio C has proposed an ingenious solutionâ€”an "L" shape geometry. Here, the hotel program takes the form of the vertical mass, while the amenity programs gracefully occupy the horizontal mass.

Comprising approximately 500 units, the hotel stands as a vertical sanctuary, while the amenity programs encompass conference centers, ballrooms, restaurants, and retail spaces within the horizontal expanse. Strategic planning places all public spaces with seating arrangements along the seaside, maximizing the breathtaking ocean views. Simultaneously, commercial spaces are thoughtfully positioned along the roadside to invigorate pedestrian traffic.

This interplay between seaside and roadside programs creates a seamlessly interwoven public circulation. The pivotal "node" at the intersection of the hotel lobby and amenity programs serves as a focal point, evoking a distinct sense of arrival and departure. This experience mirrors the inherent dynamics of the adjacent seaport terminal, establishing a harmonious connection between the luxurious hotel and its vibrant surroundings.
