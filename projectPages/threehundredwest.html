300 WEST 30TH STREET

This 16-story, 60,000 sq. ft. mixed-use building comprises 80 residential units with ground floor commercial retail. Embracing the intersection of 8th Avenue and West 30th Street, a modern design approach is adopted. Stone materials adorn both sides of the corner, seamlessly integrating with the existing context. Residential recreation amenities are situated on the 2nd floor, featuring both indoor and outdoor functions. Upper-floor residences will enjoy private terraces at the building setbacks. Each unit along the street and avenue fronts will feature accompanying balconies, providing a harmonious blend of indoor and outdoor living for the residents.
