100 EAST BROADWAY

PROJECT TYPE: COMMERCIAL | LOCATION: NEW YORK CITY | SIZE: 100,000 SF | YEUNG REAL ESTATE

Nestled between the Manhattan Bridge and the Williamsburg Bridge, the mixed-use tower at 2 Pike Street occupies a prominent location with three street frontages. The robust vertical core, housing circulation and mechanical support, stands distinctively against the adjacent building, establishing a unique identity for the proposed structure. The ground floor is dedicated to commercial retail space, while the second and third floors feature a versatile restaurant that can be transformed into banquet halls. Above, the office tower ascends, leaning against the Manhattan Bridge and facing towards the Williamsburg Bridge.
