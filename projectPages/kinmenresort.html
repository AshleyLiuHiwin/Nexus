KINMEN RESORT

PROJECT TYPE: HOSPITALITY | LOCATION: KINMEN, CHINA | SIZE: 1,800,000 SF| STATUS: PROPOSAL

The Kinmen Resort, nestled along one of the rare white sand beachfronts on Kinmen Island, is a distinctive retreat. The wings of the double-loaded hotel towers are gracefully tilted towards the ocean at an angle of about 20 degrees, ensuring that all 800 living units enjoy a breathtaking ocean view.

Upon entering the resort, the main axis seamlessly traverses through lush gardens, branching off to paths that lead to a plethora of hotel amenities including retail spaces, conference centers, multi-purpose halls, restaurants, spas, and gyms. The resort capitalizes on a 10-meter elevation difference to create a unique 'waterfall' experience, guiding visitors from the "landside" to the "waterside" in a gradual and immersive descent. This thoughtful design not only enhances the overall aesthetic but also transforms the journey within the resort into a captivating experience, where natural elements are integrated to create a harmonious connection between the built environment and the pristine surroundings of Kinmen Island.
