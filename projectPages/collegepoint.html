COLLEGE POINT

PROJECT TYPE: MIXED USE | LOCATION: QUEENS, NY | SIZE: 145,000 SF| STATUS: PROPOSAL

The envisioned mixed-use building occupies a site recently rezoned by the NYC Planning Department, strategically positioned to catalyze commercial activities in downtown Flushing, NY. The striking faceted glass fa√ßade of the commercial office tower boldly fronts the bustling major traffic street, serving as a distinctive marker that heralds the commencement of a vibrant commercial belt weaving its way through downtown Flushing.

Designed with a centralized vertical core, the building ensures an encompassing panoramic view from each floor, contributing to an elevated and dynamic work environment. The incorporation of shallow floor plates and increased floor-to-floor dimensions intelligently maximizes natural light intake during daylight hours, reducing reliance on artificial lighting and fostering a more sustainable and energy-efficient workspace.

The tower elegantly rests upon a porous plinth, seamlessly interwoven with diverse community facilities and retail spaces, seamlessly integrating into the existing urban context. This strategic integration not only enhances the building's functionality but also underscores a commitment to creating a dynamic and inclusive urban environment. The proposed mix-used building stands as a testament to thoughtful urban planning, marrying architectural innovation with community-centric principles in the heart of downtown Flushing.
