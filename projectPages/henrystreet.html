280 HENRY STREET

PROJECT TYPE: MIXED USE | LOCATION: NEW YORK, NY | SIZE: 425,000 SF| STATUS: PROPOSAL

The development at 280 Henry Street is an innovative mixed-use tower, seamlessly integrating commercial spaces and community facilities at its base, while offering residential units above. To optimize functionality, parking spaces for the tower's users are efficiently located below ground. The residential tower is designed with a subtle swivel, strategically maximizing natural light exposure and creating distinct and varied spaces for each residential unit. This intentional design not only enhances the living experience but also contributes to the overall aesthetic appeal of the development.
