NORTHSHORE GOLF RESORT

PROJECT TYPE: HOSPITALITY | LOCATION: TAIPEI, TAIWAN | SIZE: 100,000 SF| STATUS: PROPOSAL

The North Shore Golf Resort, an esteemed international standard 18-hole golf course, graces the northern tip of Taiwan, perched near a cliff that offers breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean. Positioned as a tranquil escape from Taipei's bustling pace, this resort envelops visitors in the soothing embrace of sea breezes, mesmerizing sunsets, and an expansive golf course framed by the vastness of the Pacific Ocean.

In a visionary design proposal, the integration of a 100-unit hotel with a golf clubhouse enriches the existing resort experience. Living quarters are thoughtfully arranged along a single-loaded corridor, providing occupants with panoramic views of the sprawling golf course. The hotel structure, characterized by its elevation above the golf club, gracefully cantilevers towards the ocean, offering guests a unique vantage point to savor the coastal panorama.

Within the clubhouse, a host of amenities awaits, including restaurants and banquet halls on the second floor, complemented by lockers, a caf√©, and a professional shop at the ground level. The service spaces and back-of-house facilities are strategically located in the central core, ensuring operational efficiency. Importantly, the service circulation is thoughtfully separated from public circulation, maintaining a seamless and unobtrusive experience for visitors.

This design proposal not only enhances the resort's facilities but also leverages the natural beauty of the surroundings to create a harmonious and memorable retreat for those seeking leisure, relaxation, and a connection with the stunning landscape of the North Shore Golf Resort.
