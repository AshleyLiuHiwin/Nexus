44TH STREET

This seven-story, 20,000 sq. ft. building comprises 27 residential units, featuring a ground floor residential recreation area facing the street. The ground floor units include duplexes with an open cellar and direct backyard access. The top floor incorporates outdoor recreation areas and duplex units facing the street. From the 2nd floor and above, each unit will have its private balcony accessible from the bedroom. All living rooms are designed with corner windows to optimize both natural light and views. The front facade at the street line utilizes face brick materials, seamlessly connecting with and complementing the contextual surroundings. The setback facade introduces a modern touch through contrasting metal panels, adding a contemporary element to the overall design.
